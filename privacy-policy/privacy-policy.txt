QUICKBID PRO — PRIVACY POLICY
Version 1.0 | Effective Date: April 9, 2026
================================================================


THE SHORT VERSION
-----------------
QuickBid Pro version 1.0 stores all of your data — customers,
estimates, and invoices — locally on your device only. We do not
collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external
servers.


1. WHO WE ARE
-------------
QuickBid Pro is a mobile application for iOS designed to help solo
home-service business owners create estimates and invoices. It is
developed and maintained as an independent application.


2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT
---------------------------------
In version 1.0 of QuickBid Pro, we do NOT collect, transmit, or
have access to:

  - Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  - Your customers' names, addresses, or contact details
  - Your business information, estimates, or invoices
  - Location data
  - Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  - Usage analytics or behavioral data
  - Crash reports or diagnostic data

There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no backend servers in this
version of the application.


3. HOW YOUR DATA IS STORED
---------------------------
All data you enter into QuickBid Pro — including business profile
details, customer records, estimates, and invoices — is stored
exclusively using AsyncStorage, a local on-device storage
mechanism provided by the React Native / Expo framework.

This data remains on your device and is accessible only through the
app. It is not synced to any cloud service, not backed up by us, and
not shared with any third party by this application.

IMPORTANT: Because data is stored only on your device, deleting the
app will permanently delete all data. We recommend periodically
sharing or printing your important documents as a backup.


4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
------------------------
Version 1.0 does not integrate any third-party analytics,
advertising, crash reporting, or payment processing services.

The PDF generation feature uses the device's native sharing
capabilities (iOS Share Sheet) to share files you explicitly choose
to share. This process is entirely initiated and controlled by you.


5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
----------------------
QuickBid Pro is intended for use by adults operating a home-service
business. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age.
We do not knowingly collect information from children.


6. FUTURE UPDATES TO THIS POLICY
----------------------------------
Future versions of QuickBid Pro may introduce optional features such
as:

  - Cloud backup or data sync
  - Analytics or crash reporting to improve app quality
  - In-app purchases or subscription management

If any of these features are added, this privacy policy will be
updated BEFORE the feature is released to accurately describe what
data is collected, how it is used, and what your choices are. You
will be notified of material changes through an updated effective
date and, where applicable, an in-app notice.


7. YOUR RIGHTS
--------------
Because QuickBid Pro does not collect or store any data on our
servers, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct,
export, or delete. All data you wish to remove can be deleted by
uninstalling the application.


8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
--------------------------
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The most
current version will always be available at the URL where this
page is hosted. The effective date at the top of this page reflects
the date of the most recent revision.

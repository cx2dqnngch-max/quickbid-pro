Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

The short version QuickBid Pro version 1.0 stores all of your data — customers, estimates, and invoices — locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Who We Are

QuickBid Pro is a mobile application for iOS designed to help solo home-service business owners create estimates and invoices. It is developed and maintained as an independent application.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

In version 1.0 of QuickBid Pro, we do not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your customers' names, addresses, or contact details
  • Your business information, estimates, or invoices
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no backend servers in this version of the application.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into QuickBid Pro — including business profile details, customer records, estimates, and invoices — is stored exclusively using AsyncStorage, a local on-device storage mechanism provided by the React Native / Expo framework.

This data remains on your device and is accessible only through the app. It is not synced to any cloud service, not backed up by us, and not shared with any third party by this application.

Important: Because data is stored only on your device, deleting the app will permanently delete all data. We recommend periodically sharing or printing your important documents as a backup.

4. Third-Party Services

Version 1.0 does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or payment processing services.

The PDF generation feature uses the device's native sharing capabilities (iOS Share Sheet) to share files you explicitly choose to share. This process is entirely initiated and controlled by you.

5. Children's Privacy

QuickBid Pro is intended for use by adults operating a home-service business. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Future Updates to This Policy

Future versions of QuickBid Pro may introduce optional features such as:

  • Cloud backup or data sync
  • Analytics or crash reporting to improve app quality
  • In-app purchases or subscription management

If any of these features are added, this privacy policy will be updated before the feature is released to accurately describe what data is collected, how it is used, and what your choices are. You will be notified of material changes through an updated effective date and, where applicable, an in-app notice.

7. Your Rights

Because QuickBid Pro does not collect or store any data on our servers, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or delete. All data you wish to remove can be deleted by uninstalling the application.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at the URL where this page is hosted. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the date of the most recent revision.
